> NeuroLocate - Locating the Origin of Epileptic Seizures with a Physics-Informed Neural Network
Personal Ongoing

20 million people worldwide suffer from drug-resistant epilepsy. For severe cases, the only remedy is removing where the seizures tend to originate from (the Epileptogenic Zone) with surgery, hence (as an extension of a research project) designed a:

Physics-Informed Neural Network that predicts where the Epileptogenic Zone is, locally in your browser

Integrated the 4 Hodgkin-Huxley equations (that describe the properties of the axon membrane) into the model's loss function to abide by brain thermodynamics

Lets you interact with a 3D WebGL model of the brain (and predicted seizure origin) with gestures

Occasional Paper July 2026
Why AI is Closer to God than are Humans Haberdashers' School Occasional Papers Series · Paper No. 100
  • > Argues that AI's high-dimensional cognition more closely resembles Kant's concept of Divine Intelligence than human understanding does — drawing on Locke, Russell, and Ghrist to establish the hard limits of human perception.
  • > Uses AlphaGo's Move 37 as a central case study: a move generated entirely through self-supervised learning that no human could explain, suggesting AI has transcended the phenomenal world into something closer to Kant's Divine Intellect.
  • > Demonstrates that AI satisfies both of Kant's criteria for Divine Cognition — atemporal processing (holding past, present, and future as simultaneous vectors) and modal realism (holding all possibilities in its probability space).
  • > Published as Occasional Paper Number One Hundred in the Haberdashers' School series.
